Welcome to Warriors In Pink

After an encounter with violence and abuse, it can be very hard to find a sense of worth and belonging. That is why Warriors in Pink is here, to help you find you again. You are strong, you are loved, you are beautiful, you are worthy, and you are not alone.

Sexual Violence Statistics

"Did you know that according to the 2022 Kenya Demographic Health Survey, 16% of women in Kenya have experienced physical violence since the age of 15, and 13% have experienced sexual violence at some point in their lives?"

The fact that the perpetrators of this heinous acts are rarely brought to justice is even more frustrating.They thrive on our silence,our fear.Let us stand with Warriors in Pink to fight for justice,to reclaime our power and our voice.We are in this together."
